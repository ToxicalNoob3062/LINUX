processes can be executed in two types:
1) foreground
2) background -> to run a command or program in background add (&) at the end after writing command and redirect it output pipelines to (/dev/null) known as the blackhole of linux files-system which deletes automatically if anything is written into it.
3) only one forground process can run at a time until the output comes back to the terminal.
4) many background process run at a time in background without interrupting the commandline.
5)system background process run as (daemon process ??) to provide services to others process!
6)the app we open on GUI in linux are also belonging to background process.
